Our Story

Where Beauty Meets Artistry

Shivangi's Beauty Art & Hair was born from a simple but powerful vision: to create a space in Nadiad where every woman feels truly pampered, beautiful, and valued.

Founded over 5 years ago, our salon quickly became a trusted name in the local community — not just for our excellent services, but for the genuine warmth and care we extend to every single client. Whether you're coming in for a quick trim or a full bridal transformation, you will always receive our undivided attention and expertise.

We believe beauty is personal. That's why we take the time to understand your unique style, skin type, and preferences before recommending any service. Our skilled professionals use only premium, skin-safe products to ensure the best results every time.
