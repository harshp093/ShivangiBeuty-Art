Our Story

A Salon Built on Passion

Shivangi's Beauty Art & Hair was founded with a simple dream — to create a warm, welcoming space where every woman walks out feeling confident and beautiful.

Located in the heart of Nadiad, Gujarat, our salon is dedicated to delivering premium beauty services with a personal touch. Every client who walks through our doors receives expert care, top-quality products, and a transformative beauty experience.

We specialize in everything from everyday hair styling to elaborate bridal makeovers — all in a clean, hygienic, and beautifully designed environment.
